# Ballast — Design Brief (for visual design / image generation)

Paste this into ChatGPT (or any image tool) as context, then use the ready-made prompts at the end. This document is self-contained — it does not require any other file.

---

## What Ballast is (in one line)

Ballast is an **AI agent** that helps people **outside the United States** protect their savings and access **US blue-chip stocks** (Tesla, Amazon, etc.) just by chatting in plain language. It runs on a blockchain (Robinhood Chain) but hides all the crypto complexity behind a calm, simple conversation.

## The feeling we want to create

The name "Ballast" = the weight that keeps a ship steady in rough seas. The emotional promise is **stability, safety, and calm control** in the middle of economic uncertainty (inflation, currency that loses value, being locked out of good investments). 

The product should NOT feel like: a hype crypto app, a flashy trading terminal, neon "to the moon" energy, or anything intimidating. It SHOULD feel like: a trustworthy, modern, friendly financial companion — closer to a thoughtful fintech (calm, clean, human) than to a casino.

## Who uses it

Everyday people outside the US — in Europe, Latin America, Asia, Africa. Not crypto experts. Two situations:
1. People whose local money loses value to inflation and want a safe store of value.
2. People who simply can't easily buy US stocks and want access.

Think: a teacher in Lima, a freelancer in Lagos, a nurse in Manila, a shop owner in Buenos Aires, a young professional in Warsaw. Real, relatable, global, ordinary people — not Wall Street types.

## Brand personality

- **Calm & reassuring** — steady, never anxious.
- **Clear & honest** — plain language, no jargon, no hype, no false promises.
- **Modern & global** — clean, contemporary, internationally relatable.
- **Human & warm** — a helpful companion, not a cold machine.

## Suggested visual direction (a starting point, not a rule)

- **Mood:** calm, premium, trustworthy, optimistic. Soft and steady, not loud.
- **Color idea:** deep navy / ocean blues as the anchor (ties to the "steady ship in deep water" metaphor), with a warm, hopeful accent (a soft gold, teal, or warm green for "growth/safety"). Plenty of breathing room / whitespace.
- **Imagery metaphors that fit:** an anchor, ballast, a steady ship in calm vs. stormy water, a lighthouse, a harbor, a horizon line, solid ground. Avoid literal dollar signs, bulls/bears, rockets, lambos.
- **Typography vibe:** clean, modern, humanist sans-serif. Confident but friendly.
- **Iconography:** simple, rounded, minimal line icons.

## Key screens to visualize (for mockups)

1. **Chat screen** — the heart of the product. A friendly conversation where the user types a goal ("protect my savings from inflation") and the agent responds with a clear, calm explanation and a simple proposed plan. Show a confirm button. Amounts appear in the user's local currency.
2. **Portfolio dashboard** — clean cards for each stock (Tesla, Amazon, Netflix…), a clear total value shown in local currency, and a simple chart comparing "cash left alone" vs "protected with Ballast." Plus a quiet "activity" list showing the agent making automatic weekly investments on its own.
3. **Onboarding** — pick your country, connect a wallet, all feeling effortless and welcoming.
4. **Logo / brand mark** — an emblem built around the ballast/anchor/steady-ship idea.

## One-paragraph summary you can reuse anywhere

> Ballast is an AI agent that lets people outside the US protect their savings and invest in US blue-chip stocks just by chatting in plain language. For someone whose local currency is losing value, or who simply can't easily access US markets, Ballast turns a complicated, intimidating process into a calm conversation — and then keeps working on its own, making small automatic investments over time. It runs on Robinhood Chain but hides all the blockchain complexity, so it feels less like crypto and more like a trustworthy financial companion that keeps your savings steady.
